Overview

The tool combines live MT5 XAUUSD data with multi-timeframe analysis, Smart Money Concepts, Volume Profile, order-flow context, optional futures connectors and a paper-validation journal.

Main tabs

  • Dashboard: current price, data quality, market state and final BUY, SELL or WAIT result.
  • Trade Plan: entry zone, invalidation, stop-loss and target structure.
  • Multi-Timeframe: M1 through H4 trend, momentum and volatility context.
  • Smart Money: BOS, CHoCH, liquidity sweeps, order blocks, FVG and premium/discount zones.
  • Volume Profile: POC, VAH, VAL, VWAP and acceptance/rejection areas.
  • Order Flow Lab: live MT5 tick activity, imbalance, bursts and absorption-style estimates.
  • Global Flow: optional exact futures data plus delayed macro and positioning context.
  • Validation: paper outcomes, calibration buckets, regime performance and false-signal analysis.

Validation-first design

Signals are stored in a paper journal. A setup is not counted as a trade until the proposed entry zone is touched. TP-first, SL-first and ambiguous same-candle outcomes are kept separate to avoid favourable assumptions.

Limitations

Exact global spot-XAUUSD volume is not available as one centralized public feed. Broker-specific activity can differ, delayed institutional reports cannot predict intraday moves alone, and confidence requires a sufficiently large live sample.

Leveraged trading involves substantial risk. This page documents software research, not a promise of profitability.
