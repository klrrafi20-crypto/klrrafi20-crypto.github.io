AuRa X News App

A bilingual gold-market news and education app for English and Malayalam readers.

Gold NewsMalayalamWindowsAndroidMarket Context
The goal is to make macroeconomic news easier to understand, not to convert headlines into guaranteed trades.
Download AuRa X News

Overview

AuRa X News is a focused gold-market news application. It organizes high-impact economic events and explains why they may matter to XAUUSD in clear English and Malayalam.

Core features

  • High-impact event categorization for inflation, employment, central banks, yields, the US dollar and geopolitical developments.
  • Plain-language explanation of possible bullish, bearish or mixed relationships with gold.
  • English and Malayalam reading modes.
  • Desktop and mobile-friendly cards for quick scanning.
  • Separation between confirmed information, interpretation and uncertainty.

Content workflow

Economic event or verified market headline
↓
Relevance classification for USD, yields and gold
↓
Beginner-friendly explanation
↓
English / Malayalam mobile cards

How it supports AuRa X

The app adds event context to the technical predictor. It can warn that a high-impact release is approaching or explain why volatility increased, but it should not override live risk controls or act as a standalone entry signal.

Limitations

News impact is conditional. The same headline may produce different reactions depending on expectations, revisions, positioning and liquidity. The app therefore explains scenarios rather than claiming certainty.
