About me

From investigations to intelligent systems.

I am a final-year postgraduate student pursuing an integrated BSc–MSc in Forensic Science with a Cyber Security specialization at the National Forensic Sciences University. My work sits at the intersection of defensive cybersecurity, digital investigations and practical software development.

I build projects to solve real workflow problems: understanding AWS identity risk, detecting unusual cloud activity, simplifying XAUUSD market context and measuring whether a prediction system actually works instead of only looking impressive.
