Tick Volume vs Real Exchange Volume

A clear explanation of what XAUUSD volume data can and cannot tell us.

Market DataXAUUSDCOMEXTransparency
“Real-time volume” can mean very different things. Correct source labeling is essential.

Why exact global XAUUSD volume is unavailable

Spot XAUUSD is traded across decentralized OTC venues, banks, brokers and liquidity providers. There is no single public exchange that records every worldwide spot-gold transaction in one live counter.

What MT5 volume means

For a typical XAUUSD CFD symbol, MT5 commonly provides broker-specific tick activity. It can support live pressure estimates, but it is not the exact number of global contracts or people buying and selling.

What can be exact

Centralized gold futures such as GC or MGC can provide exact traded-contract volume for the connected futures contract when a legitimate licensed source is available. This is still not the entire worldwide spot market.

How AuRa X labels data

DisplayInterpretation
LIVE MT5 ACTIVITYBroker-specific real-time tick and price activity estimate
LIVE EXCHANGE FUTURESExact contract volume for the connected GC/MGC source
DELAYED INSTITUTIONAL CONTEXTPublic positioning or holdings reports with publication delay
NOT CONNECTEDNo exact source is available; values must not be invented

Conclusion

A trustworthy tool should say exactly what each number represents. Estimated broker flow can still be useful, but it should never be described as the exact worldwide number of institutions or people buying gold.
